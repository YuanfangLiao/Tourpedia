Jade Dragon Snow Mountain


Jade Dragon Snow Mountain is called "Auroux" in the Naxi language, meaning silver rock. The snow, thirteen peaks in an unbroken line, like a dragon flying over, so called "ERON". Because the main lithology is limestone and basalt, no confusion, it is also known as the "black and white snow". Is the Naxi Naxi legend Kamiyama, incarnation of protection of God "more than 3".
