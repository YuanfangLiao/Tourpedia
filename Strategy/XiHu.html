West Lake


West Lake, located in the west of Zhejiang City, Hangzhou Province, is one of the first national key scenic spots and one of the ten major scenic spots in china. It is one of the main ornamental freshwater lakes in mainland China. It is also the only one of the world heritage sites in China today and the only one in china.Surrounded by mountains on the three sides, West Lake covers an area of about 6.39 square kilometers, with an east-west width of about 2.8 kilometers, a length of about 3.2 km from the north to the south, and a lake around 15 kilometers a week.
